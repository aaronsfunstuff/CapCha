About the Game

This CAPTCHA game challenges you to select specific images to prove you're human. It's a whimsical take on traditional CAPTCHA tests, making it both fun and functional for users.

Features

  • Interactive image selection
  • User-friendly interface
  • Engaging gameplay
  • Easy-to-understand instructions

How to Play

  1. Click the "Play Now" button.
  2. Select the correct images as instructed.
  3. Verify your selections by clicking "Check Answer".
  4. Enjoy proving you're not a robot!

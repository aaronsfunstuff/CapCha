About the Game

This CAPTCHA game challenges you to click on specific car images to verify your human identity. It's a simple yet engaging activity to improve user interaction with web applications.

Features

  • Click on car images to select them.
  • Verify your selection with the "Check Answer" button.
  • Reset the game by clicking on any image again.

How to Play

  1. Click the "Play Now" button above or visit the game page.
  2. Click on the displayed car images to select them.
  3. Click the "Check Answer" button to verify your selection.
  4. Enjoy the game and challenge yourself!
